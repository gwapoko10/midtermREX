PLAYING

Cats love to chase toys, whether it's a feather wand, a laser pointer, or even a simple piece of string.

EXPLORING

Cats are natural explorers. They enjoy climbing on furniture, investigating boxes, and checking out new spaces.

NAPPING

Cats can sleep anywhere from 12 to 16 hours a day, often finding cozy spots to curl up and snooze.

GROOMING

Cats spend a significant amount of time grooming themselves to keep their fur clean and free of tangles.

HUNTING

Even domesticated cats have a strong hunting instinct. They may stalk and pounce on small toys or even insects.

SCRATCHING

Cats need to scratch to keep their claws healthy and to mark their territory.

WATCHING

Cats love to sit by windows and watch birds, squirrels, or anything moving outside.

MEOWING

Cats communicate with their humans through various sounds, including meowing, chirping, and trilling.

HIDE AND SEEK

Cats often enjoy hiding and surprising their humans or other pets.
