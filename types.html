CAT BREEDS

Bombay

About: The Bombay cat is a breed with a sleek, black coat and a muscular build, known for their playful and affectionate nature.

Temperament: Social, Playful, Affectionate

Origin: United States

Other Names: Bombay Cat

TICA: Standard

Lifespan: 12–16 years

British Shorthair

About: The British Shorthair is the pedigreed version of the traditional British domestic cat, with a distinctively stocky body, thick coat, and broad face.

Temperament: Undemanding, Intelligent, Easygoing, Affectionate, Quiet

Origin: Great Britain

Other Names: British Blue

TICA: Standard

Lifespan: 12–20 years

Persian

About: Known for their long, luxurious fur and sweet personalities, Persians have a distinctive flat face and round body.

Temperament: Gentle, Affectionate, Quiet, Loyal

Origin: Persia (Iran)

Other Names: Persian Cat

TICA: Standard

Lifespan: 12–17 years

Sphynx

About: Famous for their hairless appearance, Sphynx cats are playful and affectionate, with warm skin.

Temperament: Social, Energetic, Affectionate

Origin: Canada

Other Names: Canadian Sphynx

TICA: Standard

Lifespan: 8–14 years

Maine Coon

Temperament: Friendly, Intelligent, Playful

Origin: United States

Other Names: American Longhair
